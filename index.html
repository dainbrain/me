Dain Kennison

I’m Dain Kennison—a name my parents drew from Tolkien’s The Hobbit—and I’ve spent over 25 years in branding, product/UX/UI design, and front-end development. As a partner and Head of Design at La Visual, I’ve been fortunate to collaborate with dozens of brands, big and small, crafting solutions that blend creativity, technology, and purpose. A gamer and sci-fi fan, I’m passionate about innovative, platform-agnostic design. Above all, I’m a sinner saved by grace, a follower of Jesus Christ, striving to reflect His love in my work and life. I currently live in Southern California with my wonderful wife and four children.
